Parallel Manipulators

Background Parallel manipulators are closed-loop kinematic chain mechanisms linked by several independent chains (Merlet, J. P. 2006). They have several advantages over serial manipulators such as accuracy and stiffness. Because the chains are independent, each link does not have to support all subsequent links and can be lighter and smaller. The lower load and shorter link length reduce the (unmeasured) deformation in the system. The shorter kinematic chains also reduce the effect of deformation, actuator error and measurement error on the final end-effector position....
